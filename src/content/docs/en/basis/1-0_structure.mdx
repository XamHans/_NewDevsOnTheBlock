## Intro

The content is divided into multiple sections.

Although some readers may possess a good understanding of blockchain networks, it is important to note that many may require an introduction to the topic. An understanding of the fundamental building blocks, including smart contracts and the Web3, is crucial to assessing the potential impact of tokens on various use cases. Without this knowledge, it may be difficult for readers to fully comprehend how and why tokens have the ability to revolutionize various industries.

The resources and the structure of this course is inspired by the Book "Token Economy" by Shermin Voshmgir and
the Blockchain lectures from the University of Munich.

## What is the goal of this course?

The goal of this course is to provide a good understanding of the blockchain and token economy.
Despite of many other resources out there, we want to provide a resource that is easy to understand and is available in multiple languages.

## Who is this course for?

This course is for anyone who wants to learn about the blockchain and token economy. It is especially useful for people who want to learn about the blockchain and token economy in a structured way.
